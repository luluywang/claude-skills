### - [ ] Lines 929, 929: "for the purpose of the exercise" → "for the exercise"

**Comment:** Redundant phrase (economics-specific verbose pattern)

**Original:**
```
If this group is relatively small or cannot be isolated for the purpose of the exercise, the evidence cannot be used in support of no substitution between debit and credit cards.
```

**Proposed Revision:**
```
If this group is relatively small or cannot be isolated for the exercise, the evidence cannot be used in support of no substitution between debit and credit cards.
```
