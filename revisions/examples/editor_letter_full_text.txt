With reject-and-resubmit decisions like yours, I typically ask the original

The repeat-referees all note that the revision responds to many of their concerns

The new referee raises concerns with your interpretation of the difference-indifference evidence from the Durbin Amendment and finds the current
counterfactual analyses limited. The referee recommends I reject your paper.

the referees‚Äô reactions that you could further motivate the need for and likely
impact of some of your modeling decisions and are not fully leveraging the

referees are uniformly more positive and Referee 4 raises points that ‚Äì I believe
‚Äì you can make progress on, I am more optimistic about the paper‚Äôs prospect
for ultimate publication in the AER. I would therefore like to offer you the

o At a high level, the paper needs to be more open about the fact that
